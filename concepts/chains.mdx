## Tokens

The supported token list per chain is large and shifts over time — LiFi's coverage of 60+ DEX / bridge protocols means most reasonably liquid ERC-20s on the chains above are routable. The canonical list is exposed via:

```
GET /v1/tokens?chain_id={id}
```

Common stables and majors (USDC, USDT, DAI, WETH, native gas tokens) are available on every supported chain. Long-tail tokens are routable as long as LiFi can find a path — quote calls will fail with `404 no_route` for tokens it can't reach.

## DeFi protocols

| Protocol | Family | Chains | What it does | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Aave V3 | Lending | All EVM | Supply / borrow against deposited assets, earn variable APY | Yield is denominated in aTokens (e.g., aUSDC on Polygon) |
| Beefy | Auto-compounded vaults | All EVM | Yield-optimizer vaults that compound underlying protocol rewards | Strategy auto-selected per vault; rebalancing handled by Beefy |

Both protocols are accessed gaslessly via MEE super-transactions. Deposits, withdrawals, and reward harvesting are all wrapped in the same `prepare` / `submit` lifecycle as swaps.

## Ramping providers

| Provider | Direction | Geography | KYC | Settlement currency | Integration |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monerium | EUR → EURe / EURe → EUR | EEA (SEPA-eligible countries) | KYC-light via Sumsub share token if you already use Sumsub; full Monerium KYC otherwise | EURe stablecoin (1:1 EUR) | Opt-in connector — you sign Monerium's distributor agreement directly |
| Transak | Card / bank → 100+ tokens | Global (varies by token + country) | Transak handles fully | Native asset on destination chain | Opt-in connector — you sign Transak's terms directly |

See the [ramping guide](/guides/ramping) for how the contractual model works.

## Anything missing?

Fibo's chain list is curated to chains where we've validated gas sponsorship economics and DeFi protocol coverage. If you need a chain not on the list (e.g., Polygon zkEVM, Linea, Mantle, Solana SVM appchains), tell us — adding a chain is a backend-only change, not a protocol redesign.
